📋 System Features

This comprehensive mining management system includes:

  • Worker Management: Track worker details, roles, departments, and employment status
  • Equipment Tracking: Monitor equipment usage, maintenance schedules, and transfer history
  • Safety Incidents: Report and track all incidents with severity classification
  • Training Records: Maintain worker certifications and training history
  • Shift Management: Assign workers to shifts and track utilization
  • Maintenance Records: Schedule and track equipment maintenance
  • Risk Assessment: Calculate worker risk scores based on incidents and equipment usage
  • Analytics & Dashboards: View comprehensive reports and performance metrics
